Data Deletion Request

Request deletion of your BillEZy account and all associated data

Overview

We respect your right to delete your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your BillEZy account and all associated information.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Warning

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will lose:

  • All subscription records
  • All delivery history
  • All generated bills
  • All analytics data
  • Access to your account

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Account Information: Your Google account association, email, name, and profile picture
  • Subscription Data: All subscription records including names, quantities, rates, and schedules
  • Delivery Records: Complete delivery history, status, and remarks
  • Bills: All generated bills and billing history
  • Pause Periods: All vacation/pause records
  • Analytics Data: Usage statistics and app interaction data

How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: Within the App

  1. Open BillEZy app
  2. Go to Settings & Profile
  3. Sign out of your account
  4. Your local data will be cleared
  5. Follow Method 2 below to delete cloud data

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to our support team with your deletion request:

Send Deletion Request

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address (the Google account used for sign-in)
  • Confirmation that you understand data deletion is permanent

üìÖ Processing Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Within 24-48 hours of receiving your request
  • Deletion Completion: Within 30 days of acknowledgment
  • Confirmation Email: Sent once deletion is complete

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently deactivated
  • You will be unable to sign in to the app
  • All your data will be removed from Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Backups will be purged within 30 days
  • You can create a new account in the future, but previous data cannot be recovered

Data Retention for Legal Purposes

In some cases, we may retain minimal information if required by law:

  • Transaction records for tax purposes (if applicable)
  • Records needed to comply with legal obligations
  • Information required to resolve disputes or enforce agreements

Any retained data will be kept confidential and used only for legal compliance purposes.

Alternative: Export Your Data

Before requesting deletion, you may want to export your data:

  1. Open the app and go to each subscription
  2. Generate bills for your records
  3. Use the share feature to save bills as PDF
  4. Take screenshots of important information

Need Help?

If you have questions about data deletion or need assistance:

  • Email: support@billezy.app
  • GitHub Issues: Report an issue

We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

Your Privacy Rights

In addition to data deletion, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Restrict data processing
  • Object to certain data uses

For more information, see our Privacy Policy.
