Q: How do I sign in to BillEZy?

BillEZy uses Google Sign-In for secure authentication:

  • Open the app
  • Tap "Sign in with Google"
  • Select your Google account
  • Grant necessary permissions
Q: How do I create a subscription?

To create a new subscription:

  • Tap the "+" button on the home screen
  • Enter subscription details (name, type, quantity, rate)
  • Choose billing cycle (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Set delivery schedule
  • Save the subscription

üìÖ Managing Deliveries

Q: How do I mark a delivery?

To mark or update deliveries:

  • Open a subscription from the home screen
  • Tap on any date in the calendar
  • For past dates: Choose "Delivered" or "Not Delivered"
  • For future dates: Choose "Normal" or "Skip"
  • Adjust quantity if needed
  • Add remarks (optional)

Q: How do I pause a subscription?

To pause deliveries during vacation or other periods:

  • Open the subscription
  • Tap "Pause Subscription"
  • Select start and end dates
  • Add a reason (optional)
  • Confirm the pause

Paused dates won't be included in bill calculations.

üí∞ Billing & Reports

Q: How do I generate a bill?

To generate a bill:

  • Open a subscription
  • Tap "Generate Bill"
  • Select start and end dates
  • Review the bill preview
  • Share or save as PDF
Q: Can I generate bills for future dates?

Yes! You can generate bills for future periods up to 1 year ahead or your subscription end date, whichever is earlier.

Q: How is the bill amount calculated?

Bill calculation:

  • Only delivered days are counted
  • Skipped or not delivered days are excluded
  • Paused periods are excluded
  • Custom quantities are respected
  • Amount = (Total Quantity √ó Rate per unit)
