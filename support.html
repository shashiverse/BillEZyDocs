Getting Started

How do I sign in?

BillEZy uses Google Sign-In. Simply tap "Sign in with Google" and select your account.

How do I create a subscription?

Steps:

  • Tap the + button on the home screen
  • Enter subscription details (name, type, quantity, rate)
  • Choose billing cycle (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Set delivery schedule
  • Save

Managing Deliveries

How do I mark deliveries?

Steps:

  • Open a subscription
  • Tap on any date in the calendar
  • For past dates: Choose "Delivered" or "Not Delivered"
  • For future dates: Choose "Normal" or "Skip"
  • Adjust quantity if needed
  • Add remarks (optional)

What do the calendar icons mean?

  • Green checkmark: Delivered
  • Red block: Not delivered
  • Orange calendar: Skipped
  • Arrows: Custom quantity

How do I pause a subscription?

Steps:

  • Open the subscription
  • Tap "Pause Subscription"
  • Select start and end dates
  • Add reason (optional)
  • Confirm

Paused dates won't be included in bills.

Billing

How do I generate a bill?

Steps:

  • Open a subscription
  • Tap "Generate Bill"
  • Select date range
  • Review bill preview
  • Share or save as PDF

How is the bill calculated?

Bill calculation:

  • Only delivered days are counted
  • Skipped or not delivered days are excluded
  • Paused periods are excluded
  • Custom quantities are respected
  • Amount = Total Quantity Ã— Rate per unit

Troubleshooting

Data not syncing?

  • Check internet connection
  • Ensure you're signed in
  • Force close and reopen the app
  • Clear app cache if needed

Can't sign in with Google?

  • Ensure Google Play Services is installed
  • Check internet connection
  • Update the app to latest version
  • Try a different Google account
