Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to BillEZy ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture.
  • Subscription Data: Information about your delivery subscriptions including name, type, quantity, rate, and billing cycle.
  • Delivery Records: Daily delivery status, quantities, and optional remarks you enter.
  • Generated Bills: Billing information based on your subscription and delivery data.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, and app version for debugging purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app to improve user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate and identify you within the app
  • Store and sync your subscription and delivery data across devices
  • Generate accurate bills based on your delivery records
  • Provide analytics on your subscriptions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • All your data is securely stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access
  • Your data is associated with your Google account and isolated from other users

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

We MAY share your information only:

  • When you explicitly choose to share a bill via the app's share feature
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With service providers (Firebase/Google) who help us operate the app

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Third-Party Services

BillEZy uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure Google sign-in
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: For data storage and synchronization
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Children's Privacy

BillEZy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, through the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at:

  • Email: support@billezy.app
  • GitHub: https://github.com/shashiverse/BillEZy

Consent

By using BillEZy, you consent to this privacy policy and agree to its terms.
