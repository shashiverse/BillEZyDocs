Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture. We also store your subscription data, delivery records, and generated bills.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to authenticate you, store and sync your data across devices, and generate bills based on your delivery records.

Data Storage

All data is securely stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore and encrypted in transit. Your data is isolated from other users.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers. Your data may only be shared when you explicitly choose to share a bill via the app's share feature.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time.
